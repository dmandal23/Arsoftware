How can AR CMS help your business?

AR CMS, will launch your website in no time. AR CMS is not just a content management system, but a complete package for you to release your next blog, news site, digital magazine or any other content powered website. You get both strong front-end design as well as a robust scalable back-end system with AR CMS.

Use as a news website

AR CMS can be used for news websites easily. Categories, layout builders, tags and rich-text media support make it the perfect solution for a news website. It beats WordPress or any other platform because AR CMS has been designed with these requirements in mind.

Furthermore, a news site can at times receive huge traffic. To tackle this gracefully, we have put in-built caching with Varnish and Redis, industry’s top caching solutions.

Use as a digital magazine

AR CMS is very media friendly. The blocks and layout support showing images in the best way. Like a news website, a digital magazine can also be powered smoothly with AR CMS

Use as a blog

Easily launch your blog with categories, articles and content with AR CMS.
