Products | Biog
Every single life has a compelling story to tell. Biog is an online encyclopedia of thousands of famous and lesser-known American artist, athletes, politicians, figures, social reformers and other folks who deserve a platform. We capture the most absorbing, astounding and intriguing biographies, learn about the lives and accomplishments of these exceptional Americans. We feature newsworthy, compelling and surprising point-of-view life events; we are the digital source for a complete biography of the people that matter. Our Mission is to bring viewers face to face with these extraordinary personalities who have helped shape our life and the world in so many ways, yet for various reasons aren’t talked about enough or highlighted in the manner they be worthy of. Biog is a biography and learning site, combining contents of facts, stories and inspiring records of individuals; bestowing truths you most likely are looking for. We do our research and double-check all our articles, you can count on the facts you find on Biog. Our editors regularly update and refine —as well as adding new—to bring you reliable information. Parents, teachers and students turn to Boig for information on an array of famous Americans of present and past history.
" Biog editorial has two core priorities "
The first is that our information is clear and accurate; the second is that our articles be accessible to every people. Biog covers biography of a wide range of American personalities and gets into precise details. To make sure we get it right, the editors at Biog do a lot of research. We want our readers to have effortless experience finding out biographies of American personalities with no effort and are easily accessible.
